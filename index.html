🎭 About the Board

The FirstOntario St. Catharines Performing Arts Centre Board oversees the operation and programming of the city's premier cultural venue. Located in downtown St. Catharines, the centre hosts a diverse array of performances and community events, fostering a vibrant arts scene for residents and visitors alike.

Serving St. Catharines and the Niagara region since 2015.
